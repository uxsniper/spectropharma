About Us

Spectropharma is a Saudi company that was established in 2019. It's core business is development, manufacturing, and sales of pharmaceutical products in Saudi Arabia and abroad. Spectropharma manufacturing facility is in Sudair industrial city, 120KM north east of Riyadh, with a plant site that covers 54,000 sq. m. The plant was constructed and equipped up to the highest quality levels. Spectropharma believes that quality is the most vital determinant of a company's reputation among commercial and institutional customers in the pharmaceutical industry. Therefore, emphasis on high quality is the seesnce of its brands and will help company to differentiate from the market along with affordable medicine prices to its patients.

Through a robust quality standard, state of the art technologies, and high talent pharmacist, enginers and marketers, Spectropharma would leverage its strengths to establish itself as a major player in the pharmaceutical market in KSA and across the globe. Spectropharma specializes in producing Biosimilars, generic pharmaceuticals, CSSD, and infection protection products through partnership with multinational companies as well as in-house development. Spectropharma will forge strategic relationships, alliances, and partnerships with channel partners to maximize its reach and prevention.

MD Message

By investing in cutting edge pharmaceutical technology, research and development infrastructure, technology transfer, and building upon talented human capital, Spectropharma will help local and reguinal health care system and patients by bringing more theraphy options with high quality standard.

Through working proactively with stakeholders such as governments, healthcare agencies, our suppliers, and other segments of society in specific markets, Spectropharma seeks to ensure broad and timely access to medicines. As the global healthcare landscape presents new challenges, new diseases, and new dynamics, it is crucial to ensure that healthcare technology rapidly evolves to keep pace. As part of this priority, we aim to collaborate with multinational suppliers, academia, governments, and society to further drive the transformation of the healthcare sector.

Our constant endeavor is to look for theraphy breakthroughs wherever they occur, leveraging internal expertise and embracing external science to bring the best solutions to the pople who urgently need them. We are keen to work relentlessly to change the way diseases are treated, through aggressively pursuing new pathways and investing in cutting edge technologies and treatment modalities it surpass and outperform today's solutions.
